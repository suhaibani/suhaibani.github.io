About

My name is Mohammed Alsuhaibani. I am currently a PhD candidate at the Department of Computer Science at the University of Liverpool supervised by Dr. Danushka Bollegala and Dr. Yannis Goulermas working on the intersection between machine learning, computational linguistics and natural language processing (NLP). Prior to that I obtained my MSc in Advanced Computer Science from University of Liverpool in 2015. Before joining University of Liverpool, I worked as a budget and planning data analyst at SE and teaching assistant at Qassim University. In my amble free time, I watch/play tennis!

Research

My main interests are in the field of Machine Learning and NLP. Particularly, my research centres on lexical semantics with a focus on learning word representations/embeddings from text corpora and Knowledge Bases (KBs)/semantic lexicons.
